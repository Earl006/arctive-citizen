About Arctive Citizen

Reimagining and expanding pathways for meaningful participation in civic life, ensuring active citizenship is accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To reimagine and expand pathways for meaningful participation in civic life, ensuring active citizenship is accessible to everyone, regardless of background or resources.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has the power, resources, and support to shape just and equitable societies through active and meaningful civic engagement.

Our Story

The Beginning

Founded by Wevyn Muganda, Arctive Citizens originated from a project funded by UNOPS. What began as an initiative focused on climate awareness evolved into a broader platform addressing governance and civic issues in response to political unrest in Kenya.

Our Approach

Arctive Citizens integrates online and offline strategies through its Tech-Enabled Grassroots (TEG) model. We leverage technology while ensuring inclusivity and accessibility in civic participation, reaching citizens regardless of their digital access.

Where We Are Today

Today, Arctive Citizens stands as a platform dedicated to empowering Kenyans to actively participate in shaping their communities and societies through technology and grassroots activism.
